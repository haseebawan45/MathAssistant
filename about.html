About This App

Math Assistant

Math Assistant is an AI-powered web application designed to help users solve complex mathematical problems. It provides step-by-step solutions, explanations, and visualizations to enhance understanding of mathematical concepts.

Features:

  • Solve complex math problems step-by-step
  • Beautiful LaTeX math formatting
  • Save conversation history
  • Resume from where you left off
  • Get personalized learning recommendations
  • Access your saved conversations
  • Secure and private account
